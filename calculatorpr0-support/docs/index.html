Secure messaging with end-to-end encryption

About the App

CalculatorPR0 is a secure messaging app that creates temporary, encrypted rooms for private conversations. All messages are protected with end-to-end encryption, and rooms are automatically deleted when the session ends.

Key Features

  • End-to-End Encryption — Messages are encrypted on your device before being sent
  • Temporary Rooms — Conversations exist only while the room is active
  • No Account Required — Start messaging without registration
  • No Message History — Nothing is stored after you leave

Security Overview

CalculatorPR0 is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • End-to-End Encryption — Only participants in a room can read messages
  • On-Device Processing — Encryption and decryption happen locally on your device
  • Temporary by Design — Rooms and messages are not persisted after sessions end
  • No Tracking — We don't collect analytics or track your activity

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

System Requirements
