Before You Contact Us

Many common questions are already answered in our documentation:

  • Frequently Asked Questions — Answers to common questions about using the app
  • Privacy Policy — Information about how we protect your privacy
  • Terms of Use — Our service terms and conditions

Email Support

If you've checked the FAQ and still need help, please contact us by email:

Support Email kulcsarkevinqpc8@gmail.com

When Contacting Us, Please Include:

  • A clear description of the issue or question
  • The version of CalculatorPR0 you're using (found in Settings)
  • Your iOS version (found in Settings > General > About)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
  • Any error messages you've seen

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Please note:

  • Complex issues may require additional time to investigate
  • Response times may be longer during weekends and holidays
  • We respond in the order inquiries are received

Bug Reports

Found a bug? We appreciate you taking the time to report it. When reporting bugs, please include:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Steps to reproduce the bug
  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version number

Feature Requests

We welcome feedback and suggestions for improving CalculatorPR0. While we can't implement every request, we do review all suggestions and consider them for future updates.

What We Cannot Help With

Due to the nature of our privacy-focused design:

  • Message Recovery — We cannot recover deleted messages or closed rooms
  • Account Issues — The app does not use accounts
  • Message Content — We cannot see or access your encrypted messages
  • Third-Party Apps — We cannot provide support for other applications
