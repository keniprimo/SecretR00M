Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Effective Date: January 2025

Overview

SecretR00M ("the App") is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (and don't collect), how the App works, and your rights.

In summary: We don't collect your personal data, we can't read your messages, and we don't track your activity.

No Account Required

SecretR00M does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, phone number, or any other personal information. You can use the App without identifying yourself.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Message content
  • Contact lists
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers

End-to-End Encryption

All messages sent through CalculatorPR0 are protected by end-to-end encryption:

  • Encryption happens on your device — Messages are encrypted before they leave your phone
  • Only participants can decrypt — Only people in the room have the keys to read messages
  • We cannot access content — We have no technical ability to read your encrypted messages

Temporary Rooms

SecretR00M uses temporary rooms that exist only while participants are connected:

  • Rooms are created on-demand and exist only in memory
  • When all participants leave or the host closes the room, it is permanently deleted
  • Message history is not stored after a room ends
  • We do not maintain logs of room activity or participants

Data Processed On-Device

The following operations occur entirely on your device:

  • Encryption and decryption of messages
  • Generation of encryption keys
  • Display of messages and media
  • App preferences and settings

Data In Transit

When you use the App, the following data is transmitted:

  • Encrypted messages — Content is encrypted before transmission; only intended recipients can decrypt
  • Room coordination — Basic signals to establish and maintain room connections (e.g., join requests, participant status)
  • Invite tokens — One-time tokens to allow others to join rooms

All transmissions are encrypted in transit. We do not log or store the content of transmissions.

Local Storage

The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Optional privacy lock passcode (stored securely in device Keychain)

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

No Tracking or Analytics

SecretR00M does not include:

  • Analytics SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party trackers

No Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks.

Third-Party Services

SecretR00M does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. The App operates independently without external analytics, social media integrations, or advertising platforms.

Children's Privacy

SecretR00M is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since the App does not collect personal information, there is no data to remove.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all messages
  • Secure key generation and management on-device
  • Automatic room deletion when sessions end
  • No persistent storage of message content

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete
  • There is no account to deactivate
  • There is no data to export

Local data on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
